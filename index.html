ERGpy - Electroretinogram Analysis Software

Unlock the mysteries of ERG signals with ERGpy!

Unveiling relical insights

Step into the world of ERGpy, an automated and open-source software crafted for the analysis of electroretinogram (ERG) signals. Guided by the spirit of ancient wisdom, ERGpy combines the power of SciPy for signal processing and PyQt5 for its captivating graphical user interface.

Timeless features

  • Automated ERG signal processing, a modern tribute to the art of ancient wisdom.
  • Invoke the spirit of customization with:
    • Customizable start and end regions of the signal (t0 to t1).
    • Magnificent Butterworth filters:
      • Low-pass and band-pass for A, B, and C waves.
      • Band-pass and high-pass for resonant Oscillatory Potentials (OPs).
  • Experience a user-friendly interface.
  • Analyse your data visually, and export vector graphics with ERGpy's built-in Graph/Data viewer.

Portable

ERGpy is cross-platform, and has been tested to work on Microsoft Windows, GNU/Linux, FreeBSD, Solaris, and MacOS.
